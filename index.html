The Humanitarian Crisis

Since February 28, 2026, intense airstrikes have caused widespread civilian suffering across Iran. Verified reports indicate:

  • Over 1,000–1,348+ civilians killed, including 168–181+ children
  • Thousands wounded, many with severe burns and shrapnel injuries
  • Deadliest single incident: attack on a girls' primary school in Minab killing 165–168+ (mostly children)
  • At least 13 hospitals and health facilities damaged or destroyed
  • Hundreds of thousands displaced; acute shortages of medicine, food, water and power

Cryptocurrency donations are being collected to provide immediate medical supplies, food, water, shelter, and support for displaced families and children.
